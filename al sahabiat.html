Khadija bint Khwailid

Lady of the women of the worlds

The mother of the believers, God be pleased with her, has exploits that indicate her position in the heart of the Prophet, may God’s prayers and peace be upon him, her good Islam, her patience and tolerance for the types of harm and tribulations that she faces while she is with the Prophet, may God’s prayers and peace be upon him, in the stages of the call in Makkah Al-Mukarramah. Aisha mentioned in the hadeeth about the beginning of the revelation what Khadija did to strengthen the heart of the Prophet, may God’s prayers and peace be upon him, to receive what God had revealed to him, so he said to her: “I feared for myself.”

Hafsa bint Omar

She is fasting and she is your wife in heaven

The Prophet - upon him be peace and blessings - was fair among his wives, so that if he wanted to go out on a campaign or travel, he would draw lots among his wives, and the one on whom the lot falls would go out with him, and in one of the battles, the name of Hafsa came out in the lottery, so she went out with him, and she was in her tent during the battle. Even if the battle ended, she went to quench the thirst, treat the wounded, and relieve the pain of the injured. When the Prophet - upon him be peace and blessings be upon him - saw that, he gave her eighty wasqs * of wheat. In honor of her .

Umm Kulthum bint Muhammad

The third daughter of the Prophet

She is Umm Kulthum, the daughter of the Messenger of God, and her mother is Khadija bint Khuwaylid. Otaiba bin Abi Lahab bin Abd al-Muttalib married her before the prophethood, so when the Messenger of God was sent, and God revealed: {The hand of Abi Lahab has perished} his father, Abu Lahab, said to him: My head from your head is forbidden if you do not divorce his daughter, so he separated from her and did not enter into marriage with her, so she remained in Mecca with the Messenger God . Umm Kulthum is older than Ruqayyah and Fatimah. Others disagreed with him, and it is correct that she is younger than Ruqaya.
