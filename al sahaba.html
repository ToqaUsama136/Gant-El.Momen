The Ten Promised Paradise

Abu Bakr alsidiyq

Good news, you are God's freed from the fire

He is Abdullah Ibn Abi Quhafa, known as “Abu Bakr Al-Siddiq.” Al-Siddiq, may God be pleased with him, was the first to believe in the Prophet, who believed him when people lied to him, and was the companion of the Prophet on the migration to Yathrib. After the death of the Prophet - upon him be peace - it was proven and the Muslims were with him by saying: Whoever worships Muhammad, may God’s prayers and peace be upon him, then Muhammad has died.

Omar Ibn Khattab

Umar bin Al-Khattab, known as “Al-Farooq”, by whom God differentiated between truth and falsehood. The title of Amir al-Mu'mineen, and he was the first to be given this title, and this is due to what people see as justice during the period of his caliphate, Umar ibn al-Khattab. Othman Ibn Affan

I am not ashamed of a man whom the angels are ashamed of

Dhu al-Nurayn is Othman bin Affan, may God be pleased with him, and he was called that because he married two of the daughters of the Prophet. Othman was the first to migrate to Abyssinia to preserve Islam, then he migrated to Medina. Othman had a prominent role in equipping the Al-Usrah Army. Othman bin Affan assumed the caliphate after the death of Omar, may God be pleased with them; His assumption of the caliphate was unanimous among his Companions, and he is the third of the Rightly Guided Caliphs

Ali Ibn Abi Talib

Are you not satisfied that you are to me as Aaron was to Moses?

Ali bin Abi Talib - may God be pleased with him - is the first boy to convert to Islam and one of the most knowledgeable companions. He is the first to pray with the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace. The love of God - the Mighty and Sublime - and His Messenger - may God bless him and grant him peace - for him, and the evidence for that is what happened on the day of Khaybar, when the banner was handed over to him, after the Prophet, may God’s prayers and peace be upon him, said.

Al-Zubair Ibn Al-Awam

The first to draw a sword in the way of God

The disciples of the Messenger of God and the son of his aunt Safiya, one of the ten who were given the glad tidings of Paradise, and one of the six, the people of the Shura. He converted to Islam when he was sixteen years old. Al-Zubayr was, from a young age, a brave knight who did not fear retribution and never left behind in a campaign. His children were named after the martyrs. He was patient with the harm for the sake of Islam, and his uncle used to torture him while saying, “I will never return to disbelief".
